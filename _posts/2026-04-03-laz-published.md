The gates are open: The Lattice Assumption Zoo (LAZ) is officially live and ready for visitors!
Here's a quick look at the current state of the LAZ and how to stay up-to-date as it grows.

## Current State
While the LAZ is now publicly available and large enough to be a useful lookup resource, it is far from complete.
We are working on extending the Zoo and the progress can be tracked on our [missing assumptions list](https://github.com/jnsiemer/latticeAssumptionZoo/issues/1).

As the core infrastructure and overall concept are now stable, we officially **invite contributions from the community**.
You can find a quick guide on adding an assumption or documenting a cryptanalytic break [here](https://github.com/jnsiemer/latticeAssumptionZoo/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md).

## Stay Updated
We invite everyone to subscribe to our [RSS feed](/feed.xml) to stay informed about novel lattice assumptions and cryptanalytic breaks.
We know how annoying a cluttered feed can be, so we keep the noise to a minimum by _only_ updating the feed for major events such as ...
- the release of a novel assumption (we won't announce adding 'old'/known assumptions to the Zoo), or
- the announcement of a major cryptanalytic break of an assumption.
